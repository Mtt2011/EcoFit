Why you need time outside
and why it matters.

Spending time outdoors isn’t only about enjoying the fresh because it’s also about reconnecting with the outside world. In the 2020s we use more technology and rely a lot on it more than ever, sometimes you will get bored despite having basically infinite amounts of content in your phone.

Parks and green spaces offer the perfect answer to your boredom, they are natural recreational spaces where you can feel refreshed and can have way more physical movement than sitting in a room for an entire day.

If you spend more time outdoors, you can preserve the park, you can use it for it's main purposes and you can become way healthier and have some fun and relaxation. There are many benefits of outdoor activity.
